How to play

Players must have basic knowledge of the Korean language as well as access to a Korean keyboard.
You can only input Korean characters (e.g. 가) or full answers (e.g. 가다).
Every game, the player is given 5 lives, and the game will automatically restart after 5 seconds upon losing.
HAVE FUN! :)
